Community

At RWS, we pride ourselves on being a community-oriented school dedicated to serving our locality.
Parents and visitors are warmly invited to engage with us firsthand.

To begin with a smooth Academic Year, we organize a Parent Orientation Program before each term wherein parents are guided about the curricular and co-curricular activities, assessments and the journey of their child throughout the year. It is an interactive session where parents are free to share and clarify their doubts & concerns.

Each semester, we organize parent conferences to offer parents detailed insights into their child’s development. These meetings are designed to be engaging, allowing the parents to actively participate in discussing their child’s academic journey. Moreover, we use these sessions to set future objectives and goals collaboratively.

Discover engaging workshops at RWS designed to enhance parents' understanding of the modern international school environment. Encouraging active participation, these sessions cover various topics and aim to foster collaboration between parents, teachers, and school staff. Join us to set future goals collaboratively and ensure your child's continued growth and success!

Our Faculty

At RWS, we take immense pride in our talented and passionate team of educators. Our faculty brings together a wealth of knowledge, creativity, and dedication, ensuring every student receives the highest quality education. Committed to nurturing young minds, they work dedicatedly to foster an environment of curiosity, growth, and achievement. With their innovative teaching methods and personalized approach, they inspire students to excel academically and develop holistically, guiding them on a path to success.

Our Supportive Non-Teaching Staff

Behind the smooth running of RWS is our non-teaching staff, whose hard work and commitment often go unnoticed but are crucial to our success. From ensuring a safe and clean environment to managing administrative tasks and providing essential support to students and faculty, their contributions are invaluable. Their attention to detail, efficiency, and care creates a welcoming atmosphere where values imbibed, making them an integral part of the RWS community.
