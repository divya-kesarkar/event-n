Independence Day 2024: A Celebration of Freedom

On August 15, 2024, Rumao School of Education came alive with patriotic fervor as students and staff gathered to commemorate the 77th Independence Day of India. The event began with the hoisting of the tricolor by our esteemed principal, followed by a rousing rendition of the national anthem.

The school grounds were filled with vibrant decorations in the hues of the Indian flag. Students showcased their talents through patriotic songs, speeches, and a special skit highlighting the sacrifices of freedom fighters. A highlight of the event was a dance performance that creatively depicted India's journey from colonial rule to independence.

The principal's address reminded everyone of their duty to uphold the values of freedom, unity, and diversity.
The event concluded with the distribution of sweets and a renewed sense of pride in being Indian.
