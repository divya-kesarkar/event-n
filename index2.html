Environment Week 2024: Towards a Greener Future

From June 3 to June 7, 2024, Rumao School of Education celebrated Environment Week with the theme “Restore Our Earth.” The week-long event aimed to educate students about environmental issues and inspire sustainable practices.

Activities included a tree-planting drive, where students and teachers planted over 100 saplings on the school campus. Workshops on waste segregation, composting, and water conservation were held to encourage eco-friendly habits.

Students participated enthusiastically in a poster-making competition, creating artworks that emphasized the importance of preserving natural resources.
A community clean-up drive also saw students and staff work together to clean nearby areas, setting an example of civic responsibility. The event ended with a thought-provoking speech by the school’s eco-club leader, who urged everyone to become stewards of the environment.
